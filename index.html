Selected Projects

Pocket

Pocket is a digital solution for today’s modern shopper. It is a hassle-free mobile application that helps users keep track of online purchases by fetching information directly from their shopping-related emails. With an embedded reminder system, our users will never miss a return window again.

Circuit

Circuit is an all-in-one platform that allows users to plan travels seamlessly with friends. Stay organized by keeping travel details and communication on a shared space. Learn, explore and discover together.

Scout

Scout aims to help high school students discover volunteer opportunites within their community. Students can easily apply to positions through an in-app portal, while staying up-to-date with various organizations in their fields of interest.
